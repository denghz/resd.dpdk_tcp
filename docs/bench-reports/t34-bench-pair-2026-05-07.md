## Notes

**C=64 small-W dip**: C=64,W=256 (0.30 Gbps) and C=64,W=64 (0.278 Gbps)
are below the C=16 row. At small write sizes, 64 connections exhaust the
32768-mbuf pool during warmup; send_bytes returns Ok(0) for most calls
during the measurement window. The fix prevents this from cascading into
subsequent buckets.

**Peak**: ~2.6 Gbps at C=64,W=65536 — within range of prior T33/T30 results.
NIC ceiling (10 Gbps) not reached; echo round-trip is the limiting factor.
