## Notes

**C=64 small-W dip**: C=64,W=64 (0.079 Gbps) and C=64,W=256 (0.358 Gbps)
are below the C=16 row — same structural characteristic as all prior runs.
C=64,W=64 is lower this run (0.079 vs ~0.28 typical); pool exhaustion during
warmup left fewer mbufs available during measurement.

**Pool transient at C=64,W=256**: post-close shows 32730 (−38 mbufs);
C=1,W=1024 pre-open also shows 32730, recovering to 32768 by its post-close.
The 38 mbufs were still in the ENA TX completion queue at snapshot time — not
a leak. All subsequent pre-open snapshots are exactly 32768.

**Peak**: ~2.701 Gbps at C=1,W=262144; large-W rows (65536, 262144) strong
this run (2.669–2.701 Gbps), consistent with T36 which also showed elevated
large-W results. EC2 network conditions favourable during this window.
